Features

It should be easy to plan

With four separate objectives to build your plan, nothing will be left behind.

Task: Nail those tasks that you’ve been letting sit for weeks in minutes!

Habit: Managing everything ahead of you requires a good base, habits keep you accountable!

Event: Events take your full attention, it’s only fair that you prioritize them right?

Goal: Everybody has ambitions, but what if you could reach them?

Focus on what matters

All objectives must be categorized into either a provided category:

Resources: Career, Financial

Environment: Social, Surroundings

Health: Physical, Mental

or a project. Projects can be collaborative or solo and have a user-selected emoji and color.

Work with your brain, not against it

What fuels our decisions? Whether we’ve made a mistake or an amazing accomplishment, it is important to reflect.

Logs: Check-in every morning, night, or whenever you feel the need. Communicate what’s on your mind and your agenda will follow.
